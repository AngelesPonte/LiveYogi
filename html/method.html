Our Method

Angeles Ponte, our Live Yogi Studio creator & teacher started the two-years Kaladanda Yoga teacher training after practicing Kaladanda yoga for 6 months. "I absolutely felt in love with Kaladanda method, I wanted to learn to be able to generate in others what my teacher uncovered in me: a complete sense of peace and awareness, a physical and mental well-being that I had never felt before. Yoga save my life and I wanted be able to share this feeling with others". After two years of training, Angie quit the corporate world and started teaching yoga and karma yoga & meditation (2018)

Teachers, Masters & Inspiration

Carola Terreri

Founder and creator of the Kaladanda Yoga Method. Kaladanda is based on a combination of Indian asanas (postures) with a Tibetan Buddhist philosophy that seeks for the transformation of the being throuth a yoga and meditation practice. Carola have studied and practiced Tibetan Buddhism and Buddhist psychology for more than 20 years. Since 2015 she meditate with the close guidance of B. Alan Wallace.

Alan Wallace

He was ordained by H.H. the Dalai Lama, and over 14 years as a monk he studied with and translated for several of the generation’s greatest lamas. Through his PhD, he combined Western science and physics with contemplative Buddhism. He is the founder and director of the Santa Barbara Institute for Consciousness Studies and of the Center for Contemplative Research in Crestone, Colorado.

Pema Chödrön

Beloved Buddhist teacher, author, nun and mother, Pema Chodron has inspired millions of people from around the world who have been touched by her example and message of practicing peace in these turbulent times. She has a Foundation dedicated to preserving and sharing Pema’s inspiration and teachings in order that they might help us all awaken wisdom and compassion in ourselves and the world around us.

The Budadharma Institute

The Buddhadharma Institute is under the spiritual and academic direction of the Dharmadatta Community, a community of Buddhist nuns led by HH the 17th Karmapa Ogyen Trinley Dorje. The Institute is dedicated to providing online educational services on Buddhism and its application to daily life
