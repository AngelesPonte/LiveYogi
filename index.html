    FAQs

    What do I get access to as an LiveYogi member?
    After signing up for an Live Yogi membership, you will have full access to the library of over 2,500 classes on our website. These classes are available to stream and download on your device for on-the-go practice.
    On what devices can I watch classes?
    You can watch classes on nearly any device through liveyogi.studio:
    • -Desktop or laptop computer
    • -iPhone and Android phone
    • -iPad and Android tablet
    How does the free trial work?
    Signing up for our free trial allows you to give Wanderlust TV a test drive without being charged for 3 days. Once the free trial expires, you’ll become a full-fledged subscriber and will be billed monthly at the subscription price set when you first began your free trial. You’ll be able to stream all of our subscription-available videos anytime, anywhere, from nearly any device. Please note, there are no refunds for charges incurred on any subscription plan. To avoid being charged after starting a trial, please cancel the trial before expiration. You will not lose access to the free trial if you cancel early.
    Purchasing gift cards

    You can buy and gift someone the special treat of an online yoga membership, you can choose to gift as many months as you wish

    If I don’t like it, how do I cancel my membership?
    You can cancel your membership at any time by heading to your "manage membership" settings or clicking here, and you will not be charged for the upcoming fee. Just fill out our short survey and select the cancel membership button at the bottom of the page.
    Can I get a refound?
    Because we offer a free period for you to try Live Yogi Studio before you are billed, we do not offer refunds. You will be auto-billed monthly or annually, depending on your chosen subscription, so if you want to make sure you're not charged again, you should unsubscribe before the end of the current billing period or free trial. Cancelled subscribers will not lose access to content until the end of the current billing period or free trial.
